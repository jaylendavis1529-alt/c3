About Us

We Teach the Class School Forgot

Schools teach biology, history and calculus — almost none teach how to learn. StudyWise Point exists to fix that one missing class.

Our Story

Born From a Tutoring Frustration

Our founder spent years as a subject tutor and kept hitting the same wall: students didn't need another explanation of the mitochondria — they needed a way to still know it three weeks later. Session after session repaired knowledge that evaporated by the next visit, because the studying between sessions was re-reading and highlighting: comfortable, familiar, and nearly useless.

Meanwhile, cognitive science had spent a century building an answer — retrieval practice, spaced repetition, interleaving — that somehow never made it from the journals into a single student's Tuesday night. StudyWise Point opened in 2020 to carry it that last mile: not lectures about learning science, but working systems installed into real weekly schedules, with coaching until the habits hold.

2,400+ students later, the pattern repeats weekly: hours go down, grades go up, and — the part parents notice — the nightly homework fight quietly disappears.

Our Principles

How We Coach

Science, Not Study Hacks

Every technique we teach has decades of research behind it. No color-coded aesthetic notes, no 5 AM routines, no productivity theater.

Systems Over Willpower

Motivation is weather; systems are climate. We build schedules and environments that work on the days you don't feel like it — which is most days.

Coach Ourselves Out of a Job

The goal is a student who doesn't need us. Most engagements finish in 6–10 sessions, and we're proud every time one ends on schedule.
