Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 17, 2026

StudyWise Point ("we", "us", "our") operates studywisepoint.com. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect when you visit our website, contact us or engage our coaching services, how we use it, and the choices you have.

Information We Collect

  • Information you give us: when you call, request a callback or email us, we collect the details you provide — typically names, contact details, and information about a student's study habits and goals. We use this solely to provide coaching services.
  • Coaching records: for active clients, we keep session notes, study plans and progress records. These are confidential, shared only with the client (and parents/guardians for minors), and never sold.
  • Information collected automatically: with your consent, we use Google Analytics 4 to understand how visitors use the site (pages viewed, approximate location, device type). This data is aggregated and does not directly identify you. The focus timer on our homepage runs entirely in your browser and sends us nothing.
  • Call data: if you reach us through a phone number shown in an online advertisement, the advertising platform (such as Google Ads) may record that a call took place, its duration and the caller's number for measurement purposes.

Students and Minors

Much of our coaching involves students under 18. We collect a minor's information only with parent/guardian involvement and consent, use it exclusively for the coaching engagement, and share progress only with the family. Our website and marketing are directed at parents and students 13+; we do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

How We Use Your Information

  • To respond to enquiries, run intro calls and deliver coaching engagements.
  • To improve our website and services using aggregated analytics.
  • To measure the effectiveness of our advertising, including call-based advertising.
  • To comply with legal obligations.

We do not sell your personal information. We share it only with service providers who help us operate (scheduling, payments, analytics, advertising measurement), and only to the extent needed.

Cookies and Consent

We use cookies as described in our Cookie Policy. Analytics and advertising cookies are not set until you choose "Accept all" in our cookie banner. Choosing "Necessary only" keeps them off and the site works fully.

Your Rights

Depending on where you live (including under GDPR and CCPA/CPRA), you may have the right to access, correct, delete or export the personal information we hold about you, and to opt out of certain processing. Contact us using the details below — we respond to all verified requests within 30 days.

Data Retention & Security

We keep coaching records for the duration of an engagement plus a reasonable follow-up period, then delete them on request or per our retention schedule. We use reasonable technical and organizational safeguards, though no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

Changes to This Policy

If we change this policy, we will post the updated version here with a new "Last updated" date.
